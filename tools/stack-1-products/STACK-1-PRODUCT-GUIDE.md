## Product 1: Scope 3 Supplier Readiness Scorecard (FREE)

### Purpose

Quick diagnostic that answers: "Am I ready to respond to buyer sustainability requests?"

### Value Proposition

Find out in 5 minutes whether you're prepared for the sustainability questionnaires heading your way—and get your single most important next step.

### What It Does

- 20 Yes/No/Partial questions across 4 sections
- Calculates readiness score (0-100%)
- Identifies weakest area automatically
- Provides specific next step based on results
- Points to ESG Passport waitlist for ongoing solution

### Sections

1. **Data Availability** (5 questions) - Do you track the basics?
2. **Documentation & Policies** (5 questions) - Can you prove it?
3. **Response Capacity** (5 questions) - Can you actually respond?
4. **Strategic Preparedness** (5 questions) - Are you thinking ahead?

### Ideal Customer

- SME suppliers to larger companies
- Heard about CSRD/sustainability requirements but unsure of readiness
- Haven't received formal requests yet (or just starting to)
- Want quick assessment before investing time/money

### Conversion Path

Score reveals gaps → "Want to know exactly which gaps to fix first?" → €29 Assessment

## Product 2: Sustainability Data Readiness Assessment (€29)

### Purpose

Comprehensive gap analysis that turns "I know I have gaps" into "Here's my prioritized plan with timelines and buyer communication templates."

### Value Proposition

Discover exactly where you stand, what gaps exist, and how to close them before buyer deadlines—plus templates for responding to buyers whether you have data or not.

### What It Includes

| Component | Format | Purpose |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Buyer Requirement Readiness Scorecard | Excel | Detailed assessment across 12 categories |
| Gap Analysis & Prioritization Matrix | Excel | Quadrant plotting, action roadmap |
| Timeline Planning Tool | Excel | Scenario-based milestone mapping |
| Response Template Library | Word | 8 templates for buyer communication |
| Buyer Communication Strategy Guide | PDF | Relationship management framework |
| Benchmark Comparison Database | Excel | Industry performance context |

### The 12 Assessment Categories

1. Carbon Footprint Data
2. Energy Consumption
3. Water Use
4. Waste Management
5. Sustainable Sourcing
6. Biodiversity & Land Use
7. Chemical Management
8. Animal Welfare (if applicable)
9. Labor & Social Practices
10. Climate Adaptation
11. Certifications & Standards
12. Sustainability Governance

### Scoring Approach

- Each sub-requirement scored on Data Availability (0-5)
- Plus: Documentation status, Evidence available, Response readiness
- Weighted by buyer priority
- Generates overall readiness % and category breakdown
- Identifies critical gaps vs. quick wins

### Ideal Customer

- Completed free scorecard, knows they have gaps
- Anticipating buyer requests in next 3-12 months
- Needs structured approach to closing gaps
- Wants to communicate professionally with buyers during capability building

### Key Differentiator from Free Scorecard

| Free Scorecard | €29 Assessment |
|----------------|----------------|
| 20 questions | 50+ detailed questions |
| Yes/No/Partial | 0-5 scale + documentation + evidence |
| Single weakest area | Full gap inventory with prioritization |
| One next step | Complete action roadmap with timeline |
| No templates | 8 buyer response templates |
| 5-10 minutes | 4-7 hours comprehensive |

### Conversion Path

Assessment complete → "Ready to actually build the measurements?" → €99 Baseline System

Or: Bundle upsell at purchase → €149 for both (save ~20%)

### The 12-Week Structure

**Weeks 1-2:** Foundation & Scoping
- Stakeholder requirements analysis
- Operation mapping
- Metric prioritization

**Weeks 3-5:** Data Collection Infrastructure
- Data source inventory
- Collection systems setup
- Historical data recovery

**Weeks 6-8:** Calculation & Quality Control
- Emission factor selection
- Methodology documentation
- Quality check protocols

**Weeks 9-10:** Gap Analysis & Improvement
- Current vs. required assessment
- Staged solution design
- Technology assessment

**Weeks 11-12:** Documentation & Verification Readiness
- Baseline report compilation
- Evidence organization
- Verification readiness check

### Ideal Customer

- Knows their gaps (completed €29 assessment or equivalent)
- Facing buyer requests within 3-6 months
- Needs audit-ready documentation
- Willing to invest 90 days of structured effort
