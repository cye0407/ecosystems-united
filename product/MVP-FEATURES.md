
- [Continue]

- Form: site name, type, country, city, floor area, ownership

- Four text boxes: Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats

- [Finish Setup]

- "You're ready to start tracking"
- Show: Dashboard preview, what to do next
